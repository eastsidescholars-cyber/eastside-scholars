Eastside Scholars

Youth-Led Excellence

Eastside Scholars is a student-driven initiative committed to academic empowerment through peer mentorship. Our comprehensive tutoring programs are completely free of charge.

Lesson Formatting

A structured journey to academic achievement.

1. Initial Inquiry

Begin by submitting a formal expression of interest via email or our application portal. Please include your preferred method of contact so our team can coordinate a timely follow-up.

2. Curating Your Path

Our advisors will provide an in-depth consultation regarding our curriculum modules and tiered assistance levels. Together, we will engineer a personalized learning strategy tailored to your child's specific requirements.

3. Program Implementation

Activate your enrollment to receive curriculum-aligned resources and assignments up to three times weekly. Our support division is available for any technical or academic assistance required throughout the program.
